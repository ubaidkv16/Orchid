Why Buy Locally?

There are so many reasons to buy New Zealand grown flowers. From overall quality to value for money – local flowers and foliage are just better. Local flowers are fresher due to fact they aren’t being flown from other countries.

When you buy local your supporting New Zealand growers and their business’. They need florists and consumers to buy NZ Grown to keep demand high and the industry thriving. Love what’s in season and pick NZ grown flowers!
